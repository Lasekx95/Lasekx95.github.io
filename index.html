About Myself

Growing up I was utterly fascinated by these worlds created within video games, the stories and how the characters were not just relatable but also the emotions and feelings they conveyed. Fast forward to Highschool, I found myself creating a variety of websites that ranged from clan forums to editorial sites for these very games. Now that I've grown in years I made the decision to attend the British Columbia Institute of Technology where I'm currently working towards gaining the credentials and prerequisites I need to apply for the the B-tech program which they offer, to establish a career as a fullstack developer.
